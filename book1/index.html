Books

I am currently writing a book titled “Why is mathematics so hard?” in two versions.

Book 1, Version A is aimed at readers with no mathematical background at all.

Book 1, Version S presents the material from version A in conjunction with two different programming languages (Julia and APL). Basic programming background or interest is assumed; for those needing such a background, going through the two introductory chapters to Julia and to Ruby suffices.

Book 2, Version A targets readers with only high school level of (more or less) mathematical literacy.

Book 2, Version S presents the material of book 2, version A with the aid of Haskell programming language. No previous Haskell knowledge is assumed; basic programming proficiency in any language is required.

The books are sequential. Readers who have will have finished book 1 can directly start reading book 2.
